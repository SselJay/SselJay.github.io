Mission

My goal is straightforward. I want to spread awareness about pollution. It is given much less attention than it's supposed to, that is why I want to build a wise community with regards to environment with a sense of responsibility or accountability with their every actions.

I am concerned with the phase we're about to go through and the much foreseen future of great scientists out there.

This website is focused to enlighting people especially the elderly about proper waste disposal, segregation and the like, because my current observation is that a huge percentage of communities or individuals are still negligent about this matter.

I am thinking of improving this website through time to its full potential and educate as many as this website can.
